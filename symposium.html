About RIT…

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology (RIT), Kottayam, one of the nine engineering colleges fully owned and run by the Government of Kerala started functioning in 1991. It offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in five disciplines, Master of Technology (M. Tech) in six specializations, Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) and Master of Computer Applications (MCA). The college is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and recognized by AICTE. Over its 28 years of existence, RIT has built a strong reputation as one of Kerala's leading technological institutes. RIT attracts the best students across the state. Situated in a scenic and picturesque location, the campus provides an environment conducive to learning and achieving the academic and professional aspirations of the students. Facilities are provided to nurture talent and innovation in students. The campus with aesthetically laid out buildings, well equipped laboratories and workshops, and a team of dedicated and well qualified teachers provides an ideal destination for the future technocrats. RIT community is vibrant with many active hobby clubs and professional organizations like ISTE and IEEE.

About the Symposium…

An efficient transportation system forms the backbone of the economy of a nation since economic activities involve movement of people and goods. Though huge investment is made on Indian transport infrastructure to realize the nation’s potential for development, the demand still far exceeds supply. This symposium, envisaged to be an annual event is named after Prof Bino I Koshy, a leading transportation engineering professional in Kerala, who is due to retire from government service this year. With the broad theme “Transport Infrastructure for Emerging India’, the symposium is aimed to bring out a vision for adapting latest research outcomes in traffic and transport planning practices and construction technology to tackle issues and challenges in Indian transport sector. Speakers include renowned personalities from top academic and research institutions and industry in the country.
